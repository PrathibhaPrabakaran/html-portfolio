My top 3 Movies of All Time

Charlee

Charlee is a 2022 American drama film directed by Jesse James Miller and written by Miller and Michael Gillette. The film stars Charlee Fraser, Michael Gillette, and Jesse James Miller. The film was released on October 14, 2022.

Seetharamam

Seetharamam is a 2021 Indian Telugu-language drama film directed by Kishor B and produced by Suresh Varma. The film stars Rahul Vijay, Rajeev Kanakala, and Priyanka Sharma. The film was released on October 14, 2021.

Priyamanavale

Priyamanavale is a 2020 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by S. Raja and produced by V. S. R. Prasad. The film stars Arjun, Shalini, and Nivetha Thomas. The film was released on October 14, 2020.
